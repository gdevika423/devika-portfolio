# Case Studies

## Embedding Documentation into Agile Sprints

**Problem:**  
Documentation was treated as an afterthought, leading to delayed product launches and poor release support.

**Solution:**  
Developed a structured documentation workflow integrated into sprint planning, grooming, and review ceremonies. Set up documentation epics, tasks, and dependency tracking inside Jira.

**Impact:**  
Reduced documentation go-live delays by 50% and improved alignment with product delivery timelines.

---

## Scaling Knowledge Base for Support Teams

**Problem:**  
Support teams lacked centralized, structured content, leading to high ticket volumes and inconsistent issue resolution.

**Solution:**  
Built a modular knowledge base with 80+ guides, SOPs, and FAQs. Introduced persona-based structuring for faster findability and operational scalability.

**Impact:**  
Reduced support ticket volume by 25% within six months of KB deployment.

---

## GenAI-Driven Content Automation Pilot

**Problem:**  
Manual editorial reviews were time-consuming, leading to bottlenecks in content publishing.

**Solution:**  
Designed and executed a GenAI pilot using ChatGPT to assist editorial checks for clarity, consistency, and formatting compliance.

**Impact:**  
Editorial review time improved by 30–40%, freeing up senior writers to focus on strategic initiatives.
