At the heart of our documentation practice are a few guiding principles that keep our work clear, consistent, and user-focused — no matter how fast we scale.

## Our Documentation Principles

- **Clarity First:** Write for understanding, not for showcasing complexity.
- **User-Centricity:** Focus on user needs — what they want to achieve, what they need to know.
- **Consistency Across Content:** Standardize tone, voice, structure, and terminology across all deliverables.
- **Continuous Improvement:** Documentation is never “done” — we evolve with product and user feedback.
- **Collaboration Over Silos:** Writers work closely with product managers, developers, QA, and support.

---

## Tone and Style Guidelines

- **Voice:** Professional, approachable, confident (never overly casual or overly formal).
- **Tone:** Supportive and encouraging, especially in troubleshooting and onboarding guides.
- **Language:** Simple, direct, active voice preferred.
- **Structure:** Use H1 for page title, H2 for sections, H3 for subsections.
- **Visuals:** Use diagrams and screenshots where they help understanding — not for decoration.

---

## Core Templates We Follow

- **API Documentation Template:**
  - Overview
  - Endpoint Description
  - Request/Response Examples
  - Authentication/Authorization Requirements

- **Feature Guide Template:**
  - What the Feature Does
  - User Workflows (Step-by-Step)
  - FAQs or Edge Cases

- **Release Notes Template:**
  - New Features
  - Improvements
  - Bug Fixes
  - Deprecations/Breaking Changes

---

## Review and Publishing Workflow

1. **Draft** by assigned writer.
2. **Peer Review** for clarity, consistency, tone.
3. **Technical Review** by Product or Engineering SMEs.
4. **Final Review** by Documentation Manager.
5. **Publish** to the relevant platform (GitHub Pages, CMS, etc.).
6. **Feedback Loop:** Monitor user feedback and product updates for revisions.

---

## Quality Checklist Before Publishing

✅ Clear goal and audience  
✅ Logical structure and flow  
✅ Consistent terminology  
✅ Accurate technical information  
✅ Visuals and examples added where needed  
✅ Proofread for grammar, clarity, tone  
✅ Accessibility checked (headers, alt text)
