title: "New Writer Onboarding and Growth Plan"
date: 2025-04-20
---

Our goal is to empower every new technical writer to succeed — with a clear path to ramp up quickly, build confidence, and take independent ownership of deliverables.

---

## Phase 1: Ramp Up (Week 1)

- **Welcome and Orientation:** Introduction to our documentation philosophy, team structure, and tools.
- **Access and Setup:** Ensure access to repositories (GitHub, CMS), tools (Markdown, Hugo, Git), and product sandboxes.
- **Knowledge Immersion:** 
  - Read core product docs
  - Review style guide and content templates
- **First Task:** Simple doc update (ex: fixing typos, formatting)

---

## Phase 2: Shadowing and Guided Writing (Week 2–4)

- **Mentor Assignment:** Assigned a buddy (experienced writer) for guidance.
- **Shadow Sessions:** Attend standups, sprint planning, grooming with the Docs Team and Engineering.
- **First Draft Tasks:** 
  - Document small features
  - Write FAQs or UI text under review
- **Feedback Loops:** 
  - Participate in peer reviews
  - Conduct self-reviews using our quality checklist

---

## Phase 3: Independent Ownership (Month 2–3)

- **Own Documentation Modules:** Take ownership of one or more feature areas.
- **Process Contribution:** Improve templates, suggest content improvements, propose small playbook updates.
- **Cross-Team Collaboration:** Engage directly with Product Managers, Developers, QA, and Support.
- **Regular Check-ins:** Bi-weekly 1:1s to discuss progress, blockers, and skill development.

---

# Growth Focus Areas

- **Technical Understanding**: Build comfort with learning and explaining new tools, platforms, and processes.
- **Content Strategy:** Write user guides, developer docs, release notes
- **Continuous Improvement:** Take initiative for process/documentation upgrades
- **Leadership Development:** Mentor the next cohort of new writers!
