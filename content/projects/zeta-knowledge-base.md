At Zeta, I designed and scaled an internal and external Knowledge Base, creating over 80+ guides and FAQs for product support.  
This initiative reduced support ticket volumes by 25%, streamlined onboarding for new support agents, and improved customer self-service.

Key Highlights:
- Persona-based information architecture for different user roles
- Modular content design for scalability
- Integration with Jira workflows for continuous updates
